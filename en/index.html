Our Services

For You

Teambuildings

Organization of teambuilding games and events in closed and open space, indoor and outdoor in the mountain or near the seaside. The program is prepared according to the client's needs.

Logistics and transport

The whole organization and help during corporate events outside the office environment - accommodation, transport, food, program schedule.

Trainings

Trainings for big and small groups of people. The trainings can be one time events or longer and strategic trainings focused on different topics.

Corporate parties

The whole organization of corporate parties, birthday events, Christmas parties- logistics, program, hosts, location, cocktail, surprises for the guests and others.

Teambuildings with a cause

Organization of teambuildings connected with a social cause for a particular region in Bulgaria - focus on nature, forests, small towns, children, old people, schools, and others.

Extreme and mystery activities

Organization of extreme activities and mystery games - yachting, paragliding, kayaking, horse riding, Sherlock Holmes type of games, Murder Mystery Games.

About Us

The Association of the Bulgarian Leaders and Entrepreneurs (ABLE) is an active community of entrepreneurial young people, who contribute to the development of the environment around them, wherever they are. We met during our participation in the Bulgarian Young Leaders Program (BYLP).

ABLE Teambuildings and Events is an organization, part of Association of Bulgarian leaders and entrepreneurs (ABLE), which is managed by a team of professionals in the area of event organization. The focus of the organization is to provide personalized events, teambuildings, and trainings for small and large companies, which purpose is to bring teams together and build leaders and specialists in various spheres.
